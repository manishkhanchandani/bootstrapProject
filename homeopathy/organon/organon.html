Organon

This book was written by Hahnemann. The meaning of "Organon" is "work". The study of the organon, clearly shows that homeopathy is a product of inductive logic applied to the subject of medicine.

The Plan of Organon
Organon - Aphorisms (1 to 291)
(A) Theoretical Part

Aphorisms (1 to 70)

  1. The knowledge of diseases (Aphorism 5 to Aphorism 18)
  2. The knowledge of medicines (Aphorism 19 to Aphorism 27)
  3. The knowledge of how to choose and administer the remedy (Aphorism 28 to Aphorism 70)
(B) Practical Part

Aphorisms (71 to 291)

  1. How the physician may ascertain what is essential at the case taking (Aphorism 72 to Aphorism 100)
  2. How to gain knowledge of the curative powers of a medicine and how to use it to cure natural diseases (Aphorism 105 to Aphorism 145)
  3. The most suitable method of employing the artifical morbific agents to cure natural diseases (Aphorism 146 to Aphorism 285)
  4. Discussion on the utility of therapeutic agents other than drugs (Aphorism 286 to Aphorism 291) such as:
    • Magnetism and electricity (Aphorism 286 to Aphorism 287)
    • Mesmerism (Aphorism 288 to Aphorism 289)
    • Massage (Aphorism 290)
    • Baths (Aphorism 291)
